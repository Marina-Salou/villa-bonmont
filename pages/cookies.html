Cookie Policy

Last updated: January 19, 2026

1. What are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile phone) when you visit a website. They help websites remember information about your visit, such as your preferences and browsing history.

There are two main types of cookies:

  • Session Cookies: Deleted automatically when you close your browser
  • Persistent Cookies: Remain on your device until they expire or you delete them

2. Cookies We Use

2.3 Marketing Cookies (Not Used)

We currently do not use marketing or third-party advertising cookies. Your data is not shared with advertisers.

3. Third-Party Cookies

We use cookies from the following third-party services:

  • Airbnb: For integration with the Airbnb booking platform (required for functionality).

4. Do Not Track (DNT)

Some browsers allow you to set a "Do Not Track" preference. However, there is currently no industry standard for recognizing DNT signals. We do respect your DNT preference and will not load analytics cookies if DNT is enabled.

5. Cookie Consent & Control

Consent Banner: When you first visit our website, a cookie consent banner appears. You can:

  • Accept All: Consent to all non-essential cookies
  • Reject Non-Essential: Only use technical cookies (recommended for privacy)
  • Manage Settings: Customize your cookie preferences (this page)

Browser Controls: You can also manage cookies through your browser settings:

  • Google Chrome
  • Mozilla Firefox
  • Safari
  • Microsoft Edge

Note: Disabling technical cookies may prevent the website from functioning properly.
