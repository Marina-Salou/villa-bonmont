Frequently Asked Questions

How many guests can the villa accommodate? ▼

Villa Bonmont can comfortably accommodate up to 14 guests with 6 bedrooms and flexible sleeping arrangements.

What amenities are included? ▼

Amenities include a private pool, WiFi, air conditioning, heating, fully equipped kitchen, outdoor parking, and more. See the full listing on Airbnb for complete details.

Are pets allowed? ▼

Yes, Villa Bonmont is pet-friendly. Please contact Idoia to discuss any special requirements or restrictions.

What is the cancellation policy? ▼

Cancellation policies are set through Airbnb at the time of booking. Please refer to your reservation details for specific terms.

Is there a minimum stay requirement? ▼

Minimum stay requirements may vary. Check the Airbnb listing for current availability and minimum night requirements.

What are the house rules? ▼

House rules include:

  • Quiet hours from 22:00 to 09:00
  • Respect for the property and furnishings
  • No unauthorized parties or events
  • Maximum occupancy must not be exceeded
  • Compliance with local regulations

Is WiFi available? ▼

Yes, high-speed WiFi is available throughout the villa for all guests.

Can I host an event or party? ▼

Events require prior approval from Idoia. Please contact her directly to discuss your plans and any special requirements.

How far is the villa from the beach? ▼

Villa Bonmont is located just minutes from beautiful white-sand beaches and scenic coves along the Costa Dorada.

What checkout/checkin times are available? ▼

Standard check-in is 4:00 PM and check-out is 11:00 AM. Early check-in or late checkout may be available upon request.

How do I contact Idoia if I have issues? ▼

Contact Idoia through Airbnb messaging or email at info@villabonmont.com. She typically responds within one hour.
